Top 5 Bucket List Destinations

    1. Italy

    Italy is a dream destination for me with its rich history, stunning architecture, and world-renowned cuisine. The energy is gives is so alluring and attractive. Whether indulging in authentic Italian pizza, exploring historical landmarks, or enjoying the scenic beauty of Tuscany, Italy promises an unforgettable adventure.

    2. South Korea

    With my passion for K-pop, it is no wonder South Korea is coming in second. It is a vibrant country that seamlessly blends tradition and modernity. Its bustling capital, Seoul, is a hub of technology, fashion, and entertainment, offering endless opportunities, from the historic palaces and temples to the lively street markets and trendy districts. In addition, the serene scenery and culture of Jeju Island add to the country's allure.

    3. Australia

    I grew up watching a lot of Australian TV and was always in awe of the natural wonders and diverse landscapes. The iconic Sydney Opera House and Harbour Bridge are architectural marvels, and the Great Barrier Reef offers unparalleled snorkeling and diving experiences. Australia's unique wildlife and vibrant cities, along with stunning beaches and the Outback's rugged beauty, make it an exciting and diverse destination worth exploring.

    4. Japan

    Japan is such an amazing country where you can experience a blend of ancient traditions and futuristic technology. Tokyo is a bustling city with its tall buildings, great shopping spots, and one-of-a-kind pop culture. On the other hand, Kyoto is a beautiful place that gives you a peek into Japan's rich history. You'll find stunning temples, cozy tea houses, and peaceful gardens there. Japan's natural beauty is just breathtaking, from the iconic Mount Fuji to the relaxing hot springs of Hakone, not to mention the stunning cherry blossom festivals. And let's not forget about the incredible Japanese cuisine! From sushi to ramen, the food is a true delight, and the streets are so clean, which really adds to the whole experience.

    5. Ireland

    I have always wanted to travel to Ireland because of my Irish heritage. The beautiful landscapes, charming villages, and rich history all make it such a special place. I especially love Dublin for its mix of history and fun nightlife. And Ireland's natural wonders like the Cliffs of Moher, the lakes of Killarney, and the magical Giant's Causeway are just breathtaking. The warm and welcoming Irish people make it even better. Ireland is definitely a place you've got to visit.

Music is My Life

Music, especially K-pop, has always been a huge part of my life, bringing so much joy and energy. NCT, in particular, holds a special place in my heart, and their music and performances have helped me feel connected and inspired. Aside from the entertainment, being part of the K-pop community has introduced me to amazing friends and has been a huge source of comfort and motivation. K-pop continues to be a major part of my daily life, and I'm grateful for the friendships and inspiration it has brought into my world.

Once a Foodie, Always a Foodie

I can't really help being a foodie! It brings me so much joy to explore different culinary experiences and share delicious meals with friends and family. Whether it's street food in a bustling market or dining in an elegant restaurant, each meal is a chance to connect with diverse cultures and create lasting memories. I also adore learning about the stories and traditions behind different dishes and love experimenting with new recipes at home. Cooking and baking are not just hobbies for me; they're wonderful ways to share my love for food with those around me. Being a foodie means embracing new culinary adventures and celebrating the art of food in all its wonderful forms.
